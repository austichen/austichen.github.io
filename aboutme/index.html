This is me.

Student, photographer, and everything inbetween.

Hey, my name is Austin (Han Xiao) Chen. I’m a university student studying Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo. I was first introduced to programming through Turing in my grade nine computer technology class. I was amazed that I could make a computer do complex things by writing simple commands in notepad. Since then, I’ve moved on from Turing and have gained experience programming in Java, C++, and a bit of HTML/CSS/Javascript. I’ve also done a few side projects with embedded systems using Raspberry Pi and Onion Omega (you can learn more in the Side Projects tab!).

Beyond school, my favourite hobby is photography. It’s a great way to escape the stressful nature of school while still finding ways to challenge myself, just in a creative way instead of an academic way. I draw a lot of inspiration from Brandon Woelfel’s work - it’s not hard to notice the similarities between my photos and his. There’s something special about his use of lighting and colours to create very distinct feelings and moods that make his photos jaw dropping.

I think the combination of programming and photography creates the perfect duo for a website. Both disciplines require the application of one’s knowledge and skill - whether in an editing application or a programming language - to create a product that others can enjoy.

With that said, thanks for reading, and feel free to email me if you have any questions or would just like to chat!
